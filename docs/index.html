Frequently Asked Questions

Is RunTrend really free?

Yes, completely free. RunTrend is open source software. There are no premium features, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. Download and use all features forever.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. After the initial sync with Strava, RunTrend works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device.

Will you sell my data?

Never. Your data never leaves your device. We don't have cloud servers, we don't collect analytics, and we don't track you. Your training data is yours alone.

Do I need a Strava subscription?

No. RunTrend works with free Strava accounts. If you have Strava Premium, great – but it's not required.

What about my old runs?

RunTrend syncs all your historical Strava runs. The more data you have, the better the trends and predictions.

Can I use it for cycling/swimming?

RunTrend is specifically designed for running analytics. It filters for running activities and optimizes all charts and predictions for runners.
